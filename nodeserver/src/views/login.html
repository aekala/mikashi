Welcome to Lyric.io

A Better Way to Get Song Lyrics

Log in to Lyric.io using Spotify to automatically find lyrics to the music you're listening to. No more wasting time looking up songs, let Lyric.io do the work for you! Lyric.io is powered by Node.js, utilizes the Spotify Web API, and currently searches for lyrics from SongLyrics and Genius

You can also use our song search feature to search for songs without having to log in to Spotify!
